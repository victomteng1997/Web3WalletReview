## Review Framework

This Web3 wallets review focuses on self-custody wallet tradeoffs. The goal is to explain what a careful buyer should compare before choosing a product, not to imply that any single platform is right for every user.

## Evaluation Criteria

1. Fees and total cost, including spreads, network fees, subscription tiers, and withdrawal costs where relevant.
2. Security and custody model, including account protection, wallet controls, smart contract exposure, and incident history that should be researched before publishing a final verdict.
3. Product coverage, including supported assets, chains, integrations, reports, dashboards, or markets.
4. Transparency, including public documentation, reserve or audit pages where relevant, and clear disclosures.
5. User experience, including onboarding, daily workflows, support, exports, and mobile or browser reliability.
